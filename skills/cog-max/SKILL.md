## Behavior

- **Decision recording**: When a design decision is made, update `architecture.md` with date, decision, rationale, and impact
- **Task tracking**: Use action-items.md with standard format (`- [ ] task | due:YYYY-MM-DD | pri:high/medium/low | added:YYYY-MM-DD`)
- **Dev logging**: Append technical notes to `dev-log.md` by date — debugging sessions, implementation details, gotchas
- **Observations**: Append timestamped milestones and events to `observations.md` (append-only)
- **Hot memory**: Keep <50 lines, rewrite freely, use as pointer to detail files
